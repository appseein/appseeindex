Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to BeatVibe. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how our application handles information when you use our multimedia player services.

1. Information We Collect

BeatVibe is a local-first multimedia player.

  • Media Files: Our app requires access to your device's storage to play audio and video files. This access is used strictly to render content within the app.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit personal identification information (PII) such as your name, email address, or location to any external servers.

2. Data Usage & Processing

All audio and video processing, including the Hyper-Beat Visualizer and Frequency Analysis, happens locally on your device in real-time[cite: 1, 2]. We use the Web Audio API to analyze frequency data solely for the purpose of generating visual effects[cite: 1, 2]. This data is never saved or shared.

3. Permissions

To provide a full multimedia experience, the app may request:

  • Storage/Media Access: To load your music, videos, and subtitle files (.srt/.vtt).
  • Hardware Acceleration: To render high-performance visualizers at 60FPS[cite: 1].

4. Third-Party Services

BeatVibe does not utilize third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs that collect your data. Any external links (such as to FontAwesome or CSS libraries) are used strictly for UI styling purposes[cite: 1, 2].

5. Children’s Privacy

Our application is compliant with the International Age Rating Coalition (IARC) standards. Since we do not collect personal data, BeatVibe is safe for users of all ages.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact the development team at AppSee.
