PRIVACY POLICY

Overview

This privacy policy applies to the WORDRAW mobile application created by AppSee as a Free service. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

🔒 100% Device-Only Storage

We strictly do not collect, transmit, upload, or sync any of your personal data outside of your physical mobile device.

Absolutely zero gameplay metrics, earned gems, puzzle statistics, or unique identification keys leave your phone. All logic is rendered offline and kept within local sandbox boundaries (localStorage).

🔍 Information Collection & Use

The Application do not request, process, or track any personally identifiable credentials. Your progress history, unlocks, and in-game shop items are preserved locally on your physical device. If you clear your browser caches, cookies, or Application parameters, game properties will refresh to defaults because we do not maintain off-device cloud server databases.
