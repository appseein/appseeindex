Privacy Policy

App Name: InPhot
Developer: AppSee
Last Updated: April 23, 2026

At InPhot, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle data to ensure a safe and transparent experience for our users.

1. Data Collection & Usage

InPhot is a privacy-focused photo editing tool. We do not require account registration.

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • Photos: Your photos are processed locally on your device. We do not upload your images to our servers, except when using the "Photo to Anime" AI feature, where the image is sent over a secure connection and deleted immediately after processing.
  • Device Data: We may collect non-identifiable info (device model, OS version) to fix bugs and improve performance.

2. App Permissions

To function correctly, InPhot requests the following permissions:

Permission Reason for Access
Storage/Media To allow you to select and save edited photos.
Camera To take new photos directly for editing.
Internet Required for AI processing and ad delivery.

3. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If a parent becomes aware of data sharing, please contact us for immediate removal.

4. Security

We use industry-standard encryption for any data transmitted (like AI processing) to ensure your media remains secure during transit.
